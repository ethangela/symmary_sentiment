Our Q3 revenue declined by 1% quarter-on-quarter and 1% year- on-year in constant currency terms. For the first three quarters, our revenue grew by 1.8% over the same period last year. We see lower traction for digital transformation programs and more activity for costs and efficiency programs. Our operating margin was at 20.5%.
Revenue for nine months increased by 1.8% in constant currency and 2.5% in USD terms. Free cash flow for the quarter was robust at $665 million and the conversion to the net profit was strong at 90.6%. Consolidated cash and equivalent stood at $3.9 billion at the end of the quarter.
